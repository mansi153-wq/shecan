About Us

Global vision, local action.

She Can Foundation is a non-governmental organization registered under the Indian Society Act, 1860, dedicated to empowering women and creating a more equitable society. We provide support, resources, and training to women in communities across the globe.

Through advocacy campaigns and initiatives, we raise awareness of women's issues — and we rely on the support of individuals, corporations, and other organizations to make it happen.

What We Do

Support, resources & training

Every initiative we run is built around one question: what does this community actually need to move forward?

Skill Training
